WHY You Should Use

It is often said that Chinese listening and speaking are the two most difficult skills for learners to develop on their own. However, due to school curriculum planning and time constraints, the hours Chinese teachers can devote to teaching pronunciation and listening often fall short of meeting individual students' needs. With the aid of SmartPinyin, these challenges can be addressed, making both teaching and learning more engaging, accessible, and effective.

What we offer

Diagnostic feedback and training lessons on initials, finals, and tones in Chinese are provided.

Speaking

The system uses an automatic speech recognition (ASR) module that gives users immediate feedback.

Listening

SmartPinyin provides both online assessments and learning programs that focus on students' Chinese speaking and listening skills.

e-Portfolio

Each learner has an e-portfolio, which allows them to review what they have learned and enables teachers to monitor students' learning progress.
