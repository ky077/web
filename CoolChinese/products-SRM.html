WHY you should use

Students

You can develop cross-disciplinary knowledge integration skills through the SR-Mandarin platform, which helps students accurately identify appropriate reading levels and supports effective learning.

WHY you should use

Teachers

You can analyze the reading comprehension performance of every student in the class in detail, assign leveled books based on the curriculum, and provide personalized feedback on students' performance after automatic summary evaluations.

WHY you should use

Administrators

You can evaluate the Chinese reading comprehension performance of the whole class or of individual students, and compare it with norm-referenced group data. The system works well in AP and IB schools and aims to help students build their learning portfolios across different stages from age 6 to 22.

HOW SR-Mandarin Helps

To provide Chinese learners with a more convenient and professional reading platform, our team created SmartReading-Mandarin. The platform contains many e-books written by professional teaching teams. In addition to graded vocabulary annotation, audiobook functions are also available. Chinese learners can improve their reading ability in a relaxed and engaging way.

WHAT SR-Mandarin features

We use AI-powered grading technology to analyze a large number of books across various fields and difficulty levels, establishing the SR Score (a measure of text difficulty). A higher SR Score indicates greater text complexity and a higher required reading proficiency. The SR Score corresponds to both the CEFR (Common European Framework of Reference) and ACTFL (American Council on the Teaching of Foreign Languages) Chinese proficiency levels. Users can refer to level descriptions to assess their reading ability and select suitable books.

Aligned with ACTFL Assessments: Novice Low ~ Novice Mid

You can read simple quantitative words, such as: time, price.
You can read vocabulary related to personal information, such as nationality, school, and family status.

Aligned with ACTFL Assessments: Novice High ~ Intermediate Low

You can understand phrases or short sentences related to daily life, such as restaurants, station signs and signs.
You can understand short notice texts, such as: advertising slogans, announcement headlines.

Aligned with ACTFL Assessments: Intermediate Mid ~ Advanced Low

You can understand personal letters written in complete sentences or short paragraphs, such as notes, birthday cards.
You can read text or instructions presented in full sentences or short paragraphs in public places, such as menus, timetables, and how to buy tickets with machines.

Aligned with ACTFL Assessments: Intermediate High ~ Advanced Low

You can read texts related to your own field, and understand the main points and conclusions conveyed in the article.
You can read common written texts and capture important information, such as notices, short user manuals.
You can understand the events and feelings stated in paragraphs, such as the emotions and feelings in a private letter.

Aligned with ACTFL Assessments: Advanced Mid

You can read and understand modern texts or reports with specific viewpoints or positions, such as news, modern short essays, standard business letters.
You can read professional texts related to your own field with complex structures, such as popular science essays.

Aligned with ACTFL Assessments: Novice Low ~ Novice Mid

You can read simple quantitative words, such as: time, price.

You can read vocabulary related to personal information, such as nationality, school, and family status.

Aligned with ACTFL Assessments: Advanced High ~ Superior

You can read professional texts that are irrelevant to your own field and have complex structures, such as academic papers.
