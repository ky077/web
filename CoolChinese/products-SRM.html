HOW SR-Mandarin can solve

In order to allow Chinese learners to have a more convenient and professional Chinese reading platform, our team has created SmartReading-Mandarin. There are many e-books written by professional teaching teams in the reading platform. In addition to grading vocabulary marking, there are also audiobook functions available. Chinese learners improve their Chinese reading ability in a relaxed and interesting way.

We use AI reading grading technology to analyze a large number of books of different fields and different levels of difficulty, and established the SR score (the difficulty value of the text of the book). When the SR score of a book is larger, the difficulty of the book is higher, and the required reading ability is also higher. The SR score can also correspond to the Chinese proficiency level of the European Union CEFR and the American ACTFL. Users can refer to the description of each level to judge their reading ability and choose the books that suit them.

Assign to ACTFL Assessments: Novice Low ~ Novice Mid

You can read simple quantitative words, such as: time, price.
You can read vocabulary related to personal information, such as nationality, school, and family status.

Assign to ACTFL Assessments: Novice High ~ Intermediate Low

You can understand phrases or short sentences related to daily life, such as restaurants, station signs and signs.
You can understand short notice texts, such as: advertising slogans, announcement headlines.

Assign to ACTFL Assessments: Intermediate Mid ~ Advanced Low

You can understand personal letters written in complete sentences or short paragraphs, such as notes, birthday cards.
You can read text or instructions presented in full sentences or short paragraphs in public places, such as menus, timetables, and how to buy tickets with machines.

Assign to ACTFL Assessments: Intermediate High ~ Advanced Low

You can read texts related to your own field, and understand the main points and conclusions conveyed in the article.
You can read common written texts and capture important information, such as notices, short user manuals.
You can understand the events and feelings stated in paragraphs, such as the emotions and feelings in a private letter.

Assign to ACTFL Assessments: Advanced Mid

You can read and understand modern texts or reports with specific viewpoints or positions, such as news, modern short essays, standard business letters.
You can read professional texts related to your own field with complex structures, such as popular science essays.

Assign to ACTFL Assessments: Novice Low ~ Novice Mid

You can read simple quantitative words, such as: time, price.

You can read vocabulary related to personal information, such as nationality, school, and family status.

Assign to ACTFL Assessments: Advanced High ~ Superior

You can read professional texts that are irrelevant to your own field and have complex structures, such as academic papers.
You can appreciate different forms of literary works, and use reading strategies to deal with incomprehensible parts: such as: prose, script, novel.
