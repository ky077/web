Why you should learn with the Chinese Characters Series

The Chinese Characters Series platform not only teaches learners the basics of Chinese with efficiency and precision, but also informs parents and teachers of students' learning progress at every stage. It is a well-rounded platform that promotes learning autonomy and supports the improvement of Chinese listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills.

Precise AI Evaluation System

Chinese Characters Scale

The Chinese Characters Scale is a Chinese literacy assessment program designed for learners aged 7 to 12, as well as adult beginners. Learners complete 30 Chinese vocabulary questions—typically within 3 minutes—and the program automatically calculates the learner's vocabulary size. Based on the results, learners receive suggestions on which characters or vocabulary to study first. This helps them make continual progress aligned with their proficiency level. Results are provided immediately after each assessment, with up to 95% accuracy.

Efficient and Fast Learning

Chinese Characters Fun

The Chinese Characters Fun platform includes more than 700 everyday conversational Chinese vocabulary words used by native speakers, along with 300 animated mantras designed to help non-native speakers memorize vocabulary. Featuring 9-second animations, AI brushstroke detection, Chinese vocabulary dances, and a variety of interactive learning activities, the platform makes learning fun and easy. Spend just 10 minutes a day to learn five new vocabulary words. The learning experience is flexible and can be easily customized to suit personal preferences.

AI Auto-Generated Practice Questions

Chinese Vocabulary Comprehension Scale

The Chinese Vocabulary Comprehension Scale is an assessment tool designed for learners aged 3 to 7, as well as adult beginners. It assesses Chinese listening comprehension and can be used on smartphones or tablets. Learners complete 30 sets of pictorial multiple-choice questions within 3 minutes and receive immediate results. Assessment topics range from concrete objects such as plants, transportation, and tools, to abstract concepts like emotions and adjectives. Results are provided after each assessment with up to 95% accuracy.

Spend just five minutes each day to build your Chinese skills while having fun!
