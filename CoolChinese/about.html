eMPOWER中文字词句教学平台

eMPOWER is a Mandarin Chinese e-learning platform that covers listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills. The platform allows teachers to create teaching materials, open courses, track student progress, and give exams. Apart from self-edited teaching materials, several built-in Chinese texts are open to our members. The advanced language learning technologies such as character hand-writing programs and voice recognition programs will enhance the learning experience.

SmartPinyin中文智能听说系统-拼音

SmartPinyin is a pedagogical and diagnostic platform of Mandarin listening and pronunciation that provides immediate diagnosis and feedback for learners' listening and speaking skills, allowing learners to lay the foundation of listening and speaking without being restricted by time and space in order to improve their Chinese language skills. Our platform also provides adaptive listening and pronunciation courses, allowing learners to practice repeatedly, enhance speech perception and correct oral output.

SmartRreaing-Mandarin中文适性阅读系统

SmartReading-Mandarin is a Chinese adaptive reading platform designed for learners who learn Chinese as a second language or foreign language, providing learners with an interesting Chinese reading journey. Learners can choose different map scenes according to their own Chinese proficiency, learn Chinese reading and earn reading experience points.

SmartWriting-Mandarin中文智慧写作系统

SmartWriting-Mandarin is an automated writing analysis and feedback system that provides writing assistance to learners of Chinese as a second language. It also includes SmartWriting-Mandarin Aid, which provides step-by-step guidelines for beginners. Through this system, learners can get suggestions for amendments as soon as they finish their writing, and obtain the overall writing score and scores of four aspects immediately.

Chinese Characters series漢字詞系列

The Chinese Characters Series is both an interactive and evaluative learning platform. This platform provides an objective outlook, for teachers, on learners'progress in Chinese character recognition. At the same time, this platform also provides fun, educational and strategic methods for students to learn Chinese characters.
