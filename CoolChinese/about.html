eMPOWER中文字词句教学平台

eMPOWER is a Mandarin Chinese e-learning platform that covers listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills. The platform allows teachers to create teaching materials, offer courses, track student progress, and administer exams. In addition to self-created teaching materials, several built-in Chinese texts are available to our members. Advanced language learning technologies, such as character handwriting programs and voice recognition software, enhance the learning experience.

SmartTalking-Mandarin中文智能口说系统

SmartTalking-Mandarin is an AI-powered platform designed to help learners improve their Mandarin speaking and listening skills through interactive role-plays, themed dialogues, and simulated conversations. Real-time speech feedback enables learners to speak more accurately and naturally.

The platform also features adaptive listening and speaking assessments, offering instant analysis, personalized feedback, and clearly defined proficiency levels.

SmartPinyin中文智能听说系统-拼音

SmartPinyin is a pedagogical and diagnostic platform for Mandarin listening and pronunciation. It provides immediate diagnosis and feedback on learners' listening and speaking skills, allowing them to build a strong foundation without being restricted by time or location. The platform also offers adaptive listening and pronunciation courses, enabling learners to practice repeatedly, improve speech perception, and correct oral output.

SmartReaing-Mandarin中文适性阅读系统

SmartReading-Mandarin is a Chinese adaptive reading platform designed for learners of Chinese as a second or foreign language. It offers an engaging reading journey, allowing learners to choose different map scenes based on their proficiency level, improve their reading skills, and earn reading experience points.

 Watch Introduction Video

SmartWriting-Mandarin中文智慧写作系统

SmartWriting-Mandarin is an automated writing analysis and feedback system that provides writing assistance to learners of Chinese as a second language. It also includes SmartWriting-Mandarin Aid, which offers step-by-step guidelines for beginners. Through this system, learners receive suggestions for revisions as soon as they complete their writing and instantly obtain an overall writing score, along with scores for four specific aspects.

Chinese Characters series汉字词系列

The Chinese Characters Series is both an interactive and evaluative learning platform. It provides teachers with an objective view of learners' progress in Chinese character recognition. At the same time, it offers fun, educational, and strategic methods for students to learn Chinese characters.
