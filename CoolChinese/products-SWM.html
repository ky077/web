SmartWriting-Mandarin

中文智慧写作系统
START

WHY you should use

For learners, writing is often the most challenging skill. Learners not only need to organize a paragraph using appropriate vocabulary and correct grammar, but also must maintain coherence and unity within the paragraph or composition.

For teachers, more time is often spent correcting learners' grammatical and vocabulary errors. As a result, finding effective ways to improve students' writing skills and enhance teaching quality becomes a critical issue.

WHAT SmartWriting-Mandarin Offers

Automatic Scoring

When learners complete their writing, they receive suggestions for revisions and immediately obtain an overall writing score, along with scores for four specific aspects.

Error Correction Recommendations

SmartWriting-Mandarin can detect errors in writing and provide users with recommendations for corrections.

WHO should use SmartWriting-Mandarin

For learners

  • Use appropriate vocabulary and correct grammar
  • Apply proper collocations and maintain coherence
  • Understand paragraph structure and organization

For teachers

  • Correct grammar and vocabulary errors
  • Apply adaptive teaching strategies
  • Build student learning portfolios
