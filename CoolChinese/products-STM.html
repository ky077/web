WHY You Should Use

Listening and speaking are the hardest parts of learning Chinese—especially when you don’t have enough chances to practice. In the classroom, there’s often not enough time for everyone to speak. Outside of class, it’s hard to find someone to talk to in Chinese.

SmartTalking-Mandarin gives you the opportunity to practice real conversations anytime, anywhere. With AI-powered dialogue, you can choose fun and useful topics—like school life, travel, or daily situations—and talk freely with the system.

WHAT SmartTalking-Mandarin features

Listen to the text

Learners can adjust playback speed and follow along with pinyin and color-coded text to support comprehension. Sentence-by-sentence playback allows repeated listening and reinforces basic listening and sound recognition skills.

Role-Play Reading

Through character-based reading practice set in real-life contexts, learners record their voice and receive instant feedback on both pronunciation and listening, helping them improve step by step.

AI Chat

The platform simulates real-life conversation scenarios with a variety of themes. Learners can interact freely with the AI, practice natural responses, and strengthen real-world speaking skills.

Learning Portfolio

The system automatically records practice history and pronunciation performance on role-play reading exercises, and provides ACTFL-aligned speaking level evaluations. Learners can clearly track their growth in speaking and listening skills to support self-paced improvement.
