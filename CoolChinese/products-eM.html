WHY You Should Use

Language teachers are always committed to collecting useful materials to increase students' interest in learning languages. eMPOWER enables teachers to plan lessons efficiently. With our adaptive learning module, learners can practice Chinese language skills anytime, anywhere, and immediately check their learning outcomes on their own.

Create Your Own Materials

Use Authoring Tools to create and customize your own teaching materials. The completed editing structure includes five sections: Warm-up, Text, Characters & Words, Sentence Patterns, and Assessment. The differentiated and country-specific Chinese textbooks can be tailored to meet the diverse needs of students.

Chinese Character Handwriting Diagnosis and Remedial Instruction (CHDRI)

CHDRI can accurately assess the strokes and stroke order of more than 6,000 Chinese characters. Learners can practice writing characters using hints and guidance provided by CHDRI.

Automatic Chinese Speech Recognition Module (ASR)

ASR recognizes students' pronunciation at three levels: characters, words, and sentences. It also identifies pronunciation problems and helps students to self-correct and practice.

Recommended Chinese Teaching Materials

Smartbooks, a set of Mandarin textbooks for all Learners, were constructed to meet various degrees of language proficiency, with seven levels ranging from pre-A1 to C2. Students can acquire practical Chinese from a diverse range of topics based on real-life contexts. All registered teachers can use the texts freely. Find out more details in eMPOWER.
